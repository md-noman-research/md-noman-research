### 🧬 Research Vision
My research focuses on the intersection of **Crop Genetics**, **Stress Physiology**, and **Digital Agriculture**. I specialize in developing mechanistic simulation frameworks and multi-method statistical audits (WLS, GLM, NLS) to evaluate the stochastic resilience of cotton landraces in salt-affected deltaic regions of Bangladesh.

### 🛠 Tech Stack & Methodologies
*   **Computational Frameworks**: Monte Carlo Simulation Engines, AR(1) Environmental Modeling.
*   **Statistical Auditing**: Weighted Least Squares (WLS), Gamma GLM (Log-link), Non-linear Least Squares (NLS) Mechanistic Fitting.
*   **Domain Expertise**: Ionic Loading Analysis, Metabolic Damage Quantification, Genotype-Specific Adaptability.
